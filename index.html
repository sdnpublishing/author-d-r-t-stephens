The Author

About D.R. T STEPHENS

DT

D.R. T STEPHENS

D.R. T Stephens is one of the most wide-ranging non-fiction authors publishing today, with titles spanning quantum physics, ancient history, occult traditions, sound healing, and the frontiers of human knowledge.

With a deep curiosity about both the scientific and the metaphysical, Stephens writes for readers who refuse to be confined to a single shelf. Each book is a doorway — rigorously researched, accessibly written, and designed to leave you seeing the world differently.

From the trenches of the First World War to the architecture of astral realms, from nanotech to shamanic drumming, the only thread connecting these books is a relentless drive to understand.
